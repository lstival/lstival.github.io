### Self-Supervised Learning

I explore SSL frameworks that enable models to learn meaningful representations from unlabeled data. This involves developing training strategies that capture:

- **Temporal dependencies** in sequential data
- **Cross-modal relations** between different data types
- **Contextual patterns** without manual annotations
- **Invariant features** across transformations

By leveraging contrastive learning, masked modeling, and other SSL paradigms, I aim to create models that autonomously discover structure in diverse data sources.

I investigate methods for learning from multiple data modalities simultaneously:

- **Time Series + Metadata** - Temporal patterns with contextual information
- **Spectral + Spatial Data** - Remote sensing and multispectral analysis
- **Audio + Visual** - Multimodal perception and fusion

The goal is to develop unified representations that capture complementary information across modalities.
